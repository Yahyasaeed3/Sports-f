# User Guide
Type your preferred location into the search bar. Maps instantly shift to that area, pinpointing sports facilities nearby. Everything shows up clearly on the screen.
 The integrated table lists facility names alongside short, helpful summaries. Choosing the right spot becomes simple, and you gain total clarity before heading out. 
 Users evaluate each unique description, deciding which local venue fits their lifestyle best. Finding active spaces has never been easier than this.

Visitors land on a clean entrance page explaining the site purpose, available options and navigation paths. Everything is efficient.

Selecting a path triggers an instant redirect to the requested destination. Finding exactly what you need is very simple. 

Opting for find facility type lets you pick specific venues like a gym, football pitch, swimming pool, or tennis court. Clicking find on map reveals a city view where you pinpoint local sports spots near home or work. Once you click a icon, details appear regarding the name, precise location, and feature lists. Mapping provides the fastest route to discovery for busy athletes. Navigating these tools feels natural for anyone searching for a place to train, play, or swim without unnecessary friction during their search.
