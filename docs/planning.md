# Project Proposal

## Business Case
This report shows how crucial it is to make an app that will help people and consumers easily and quickly find sports facilities. An easy way to find places that are good to play sports is needed because there is a growing interest in fun activities as well as fitness. This proposal lays out a clear plan that includes figuring out the main problems, business benefits possible risks and the project's scope. To make sure the app works well and is easy to use it needs a strict planning strategy. This will give users a useful tool for finding sports facilities.



### Problem statement
Finding the right sports facilities is hard for users and this is the main problem that our app tries to solve. There is a growing number in people partaking in fitness and sports facilities so finding a sports facility that meets the consumers and users needs is important. Enjoying activities without having to wait at the most convenient sports facilities that are near to the user is what this app does. This app will be very useful for people who are active because fitness and health are becoming more important in modern life.


### Business benefits
1)For the app to be successful the importance of it is something you need to know. These are the main benefits of the app.2)Proximity Alerts: Consumers and users will be able to look for and find the nearest sports facilities which will save them time and effort in finding the right places.3)Compatibility with Facility Types Sports facilities that include things like gyms, badminton, basketball courts, and swimming pools, this app will help the users find that.4)Real time Availability The sports facilities availability will be shown on the app making it easy for users to see if the venue is open or fully booked.5)Cost Transparency: The sports facilities have prices you have to pay in order to use them and the app will let the users know about any fees that are required this makes planning ahead easier.6)Activity Planning Users can plan their activities by finding sports venues near them and setting up times to visit them which makes sure that everything goes smoothly.7)These features are meant to make it easy and smooth for people to find sports facilities.

### Alternatives Considered
Analysis of Current Competitors: Something we did was  look at other sports facility apps in the market and analyse them this enabled us to look at the strengths and weakness 

Facility Compatibility:  Allowing consumer and users to find venues that suit their needs such as pools and court and gyms are crucial and this app needs to ensure compatibility 

Availability of Sports Facilities:  For users to easily check if the venue is fully booked or available real time availability is crucial 

Cost Information: Providing transparent information on any charges for using a sports facility will help avoid surprises and assist users in planning ahead.

Reservation Feature: Users may appreciate the option to book a sports facility in advance especially during peak times or at popular locations.

### Potential risks
The creation of the mobile app its integration with key data sources and its communication with other systems such as maps and payment systems is what the project will focus on. Key aspects are as follows:

App Development: The main user interface will be in the form of a mobile app.

Data Integration: The mobile app will be integrated with publicly available data such as Bristol's open data for sports facility locations.

Payment and Booking Features: The software will offer a unified experience allowing users to view facility locations and pay for bookings if required.

These functions will support each other to ensure the app performs effectively providing sports enthusiasts with real time information and helping them save time while finding and booking ideal sports facilities.

## Project Scope
Visualizing the sports facilities system reveals the core components that drives this platform toward success. Each component integrates seamlessly feeding data directly into the user experience So places to play can be found by the locals. .
